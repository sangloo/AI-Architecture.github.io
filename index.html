Background

  • Baccalaureate in Mathematical Sciences – Morocco – 2010
  • Bachelor of Architecture - Bahçeşehir University – 2014
  • Master (with Thesis) Architecture - Bahçeşehir University - 2016

1. People: How architecture affects their experiences? My architecture is made around people; each design is an individual's or organization’s story. People delegate me to realize some of their thoughts; to transform that into a built form, I take that seriously. Client is the soul of the project, my main aim is to express his/hers ethics and eccentricity through design.

2. Experience: I design to enrich the different experiences of those the buildings serve. My architecture is more about experiences that is expressed by every single element of the building, small or big, structural or decorative, an experience that creates the architecture of spaces.

3. Environment: Buildings are social presences, and architecture cannot live and thrive without its environment. I endeavor concerning my supreme of crafting an architecture that adapts, however upholds persona and appeal; that blends in through standing out! Over identifying the unique aspects of each site’s, I had refined the skill of assigning this abstract spirit into a formal reaction.

4. Details: As a devoted perfectionist I believe that details generate the individuality in design.
The fundamentals of architecture and design lie in creativity and in the mastering of craftsmanship. Together, with enhancing budgets, orchestrating schedules and constructing technically impeccable buildings are aspects of beauty.
